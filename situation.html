01 · The Brief

What they needed

FaithStream is Africa's first faith-based streaming platform. They came to me with a brief for a romantic comedy. They wanted two different visual directions, each strong enough to stand on its own.

The Story
A woman is trying to escape family pressure at a gathering. In the moment, she says she is engaged to a complete stranger standing right there. The problem is, the lie starts to feel real.
Rom-Com Nigerian Gen Z
The Ask
Two poster ideas with different looks and moods. Each one had to work for both portrait posters and wide billboards. It had to feel Nigerian, feel current, and tell the story in its own way.
